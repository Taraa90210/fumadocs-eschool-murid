## Creating a New User and a New Business

Below are the steps to register on NoBox.AI by creating a new business:

1. Open your browser and visit [id.nobox.ai](https://id.nobox.ai/).

2. Click **"[Don't have an account? Register]"**.

3. Complete the registration form with your personal information.

4. Select **"Create a new business"** if you want to create a new business account.

5. Enter the required information about your business.

6. Click the **"[Register]"** button to submit your registration form.

7. Open your email inbox and look for a message from NoBox.AI. Click the link in the email to verify your account.  
   If you do not find the verification email in your main inbox, please check your spam or promotions folder.

If you want to join an existing business, you can follow the steps to create a new account and join an existing business by  
[clicking here](https://crm.nobox.ai/knowledge-base/article/membuat-user-baru-dan-bergabung-ke-perusahaan-yang-sudah-ada).

## Registering and Joining an Existing Business

Below are the steps to register and join an existing business:

1. Open your browser and visit  
   [id.nobox.ai](https://id.nobox.ai/Account/Login/?ReturnUrl=%2F).

2. Click **[Don't have an account? Register]** to start the registration process.

3. Fill out the registration form with the required personal information, such as your name, email address, and password.  
   Make sure all information is entered accurately.

4. Select **"Join an existing business"**.

5. Enter the invitation code provided by the business you want to join.  
   Make sure the code is correct and valid.

6. After completing all required information and entering the invitation code, click **[Register]** to submit your registration.

7. Once the registration is successfully submitted, check your email inbox for a verification email from NoBox.AI.  
   Click the link in the email to verify your account.

If all the steps above are completed correctly, you should be able to join the existing business on the NoBox.AI platform.  
If you encounter any issues or do not receive the verification email, please check your spam folder or contact NoBox.AI customer support for further assistance.
