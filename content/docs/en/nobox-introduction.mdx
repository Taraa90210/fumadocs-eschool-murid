NoBox Desktop is a digital platform application designed to help modern marketing teams generate more targeted customer prospects from 700+ of the world’s best business directories and classified ad websites, then broadcast promotional messages across multiple channels including WhatsApp, Telegram, Email, Shopee, Facebook, TikTok, and more. Broadcast results can be easily monitored, classified, and followed up to support sales achievement.

## **Features and Advantages of NoBox Desktop**

The main features available in NoBox Desktop are Automation and Extractor.

1. **Automation:** A feature used to broadcast promotional messages to multiple channels such as WhatsApp, Telegram, Email, Instagram, and more.
2. **Extractor:** A feature used to generate targeted customer prospects from 700+ business directory and classified ad websites such as Google Maps, OLX, and others.

## **NoBox Desktop Software Services**

1. **Broadcast & Automation**\
   NoBox is currently one of the most complete and user-friendly systems for collecting promotional broadcasts and monitoring results across modern marketing channels such as WhatsApp, Telegram, and more.
2. **Contacts Extractor**\
   With Chrome browser support, NoBox Desktop enables fast and high-quality extraction of business prospect data from various websites across different countries. The software can extract multiple data fields including links, business names, addresses, phone numbers, websites, ratings, total views, operating hours, image links, latitude, longitude, plus codes, and business categories—such as Google Maps Extractor, OLX Sites Extractor, and others.
