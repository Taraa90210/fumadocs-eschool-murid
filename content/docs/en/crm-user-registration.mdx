1. Open the website [crm.nobox.ai](https://crm.nobox.ai/)

2. Log in using an existing account, or register a new account by clicking **[Register]**.

3. Enter your Name, Email Address, Password, Company Information, then click **[Register]**.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/Register.png"
  alt="NoBox Kanban Interface"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4"
/>

4. After that, a notification will appear indicating that an account verification email has been sent.

5. Open your email inbox and click the verification link. You will be redirected to the NoBox.AI CRM login page.

6. Enter the registered Email and Password to access the main page of NoBox.AI CRM.
