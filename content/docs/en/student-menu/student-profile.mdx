# Student Profile

To view detailed student personal information, follow these steps:

1. Open the **eSchool Mobile** app, then go to the main page.
2. At the top, you will see the **student identity card** complete with name, class, and attendance number.
3. Tap the **right arrow icon (›)** on the card to enter the **Profile** page.

***

## Displayed Information:

* **Student Photo and Full Name**
* **Registration Number (NISN / NIPD)** – can be **copied** with a single tap
* **School** – school name and code
* **Class & Major**
* **Attendance**
* **Date of Birth**
* **Address** – when clicked, it will open directly in **Google Maps**

***

_Note:_ If there are discrepancies in the data, please report them immediately to the school for updates through the eSchool admin.
