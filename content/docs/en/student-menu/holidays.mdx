The **Holidays** feature in the eSchool application provides complete information about established school holidays. With an interactive calendar view and clear descriptions, students can better prepare their activities during holidays.

## Key Features:

* **Interactive Monthly Calendar**\
  Appears as a full-month calendar, with holiday dates marked in solid red for easy visual recognition.
* **Month Navigation**\
  Students can view holidays in other months using the left-right navigation buttons at the top of the calendar.
* **Holiday Details**\
  Below the calendar, detailed information about holiday date ranges and reasons is displayed.\
  Example:\
  `30-07-2025 ~ 07-08-2025`\
  **Independence Day Preparation Holiday**
* **Simple and Informative**\
  Information is arranged concisely yet thoroughly, making it easy for students to understand holiday durations without having to browse through many menus.
