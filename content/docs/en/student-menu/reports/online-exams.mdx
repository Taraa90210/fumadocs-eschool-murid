After selecting a subject from the Reports page, users can switch to the **Online Exams** tab to view the recapitulation data of the exam results the student has taken online.

#### **Exam Statistics**

This section displays the student's performance in taking exams:

* **Taken:** The number of exams completed by the student.
* **Missed:** The number of exams skipped or not taken.
* **Total:** The overall number of scheduled exams.

Example:

* Taken: 1
* Missed: 1
* Total: 2

#### **Grade Statistics**

Shows the total points obtained from all online exams along with its percentage relative to the maximum score.

* **Grade Obtained:** Total score from taken exams.
* **Percentage:** Ratio of grade obtained to the total maximum grade.
* **Total Grade:** Accumulation of maximum points from all exams.

Example:

* Obtained: 200
* Percentage: 25.00%
* Total: 800

#### **Exam Details**

This section presents a complete list of each assigned exam, complete with:

* **Exam Title**
* **Score obtained compared to total maximum score**
* **Grade percentage in a visual progress bar**

Example:

* **Odd Semester Physical Fitness Exam:** 200 / 500 (40%)
* **Calculus Exam:** 0 / 300 (0%)
