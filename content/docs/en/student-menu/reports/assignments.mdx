### **Assignments â€“ Assignment Activity Report**

After selecting a subject, users will be directed to the report page with two tabs: **Assignments** and **Online Exams**. The **Assignments** tab specifically presents a recap of the tasks that have been assigned and completed by the student.

#### **Assignment Statistics**

This section provides a general overview regarding:

* **Submitted Assignments:** Displays the total number of assignments successfully submitted by the student.
* **Pending Assignments:** The number of assignments not yet completed or past the deadline.
* **Total Assignments:** The overall number of assignments given by the teacher for that subject.

Example data:

* Submitted: 5
* Pending: 0
* Total: 5

#### **Grade Statistics**

This section displays assignment grade achievements in the form of:

* **Grades Obtained:** Accumulation of scores from graded assignments.
* **Grade Percentage:** Comparison of grades obtained against the total maximum grade of all assignments.
* **Total Grade:** The maximum score achievable from all assignments.

Example data:

* Obtained: 67
* Percentage: 14.26%
* Total: 470

#### **Assignment Grade Details**

Displayed as a list of individual assignments complete with:

* **Assignment Title**
* **Score obtained vs. maximum**
* **Grade percentage progress bar** (visual horizontal bar)

Example:

* **Paper Assignment:** 0 / 100 (0%)
* **PE Assignment:** 0 / 100 (0%)
