# Guardian Details Page

The **Guardian Details** page displays comprehensive information about the parent or guardian connected to the student's account.

## The information displayed includes:

1. **Guardian Name**\
   Displayed at the top with a role label as **Guardian**.
2. **Email**\
   The official email address used by the guardian to login and receive notifications from the school.\
   A copy button (ðŸ“‹) is available to quickly copy the email.
3. **Contact / Phone Number**\
   The guardian's active phone number.\
   A copy button is also available to facilitate copying the contact.

This page is useful for verifying connected parent/guardian data, especially in the context of communication between the school and the student's family.
