### Doing and Submitting Assignments

To work on an assignment in eSchool Mobile, follow these steps:

1. On the assignment details page, you will see:
   * Subject name
   * Maximum grade
   * Submission deadline
   * Description or instructions from the teacher
2. Press the **Submit Assignment** button in the bottom right corner to open the submission form.
3. Type your answer in the provided column (max. 4096 characters) or upload the assignment as a document if required.
4. Press the **Send** button to finish the submission process.

Once an assignment is sent, **you cannot change the answer**.

7. After successful submission, the assignment status will change to **Turned In** with a **Pending** label.
8. If the assignment is accepted, the status will change to **Received** with **comments** and the **grade** obtained.
9. You can view again:
   * The answer that has been sent
   * Comments or feedback from the teacher (if available)

***

This assignment feature makes it easy for students to complete assignments on time and ensures all processes are well-recorded directly from the mobile application.

If you experience issues while sending an assignment, contact the school or visit the eSchool Help Page.
