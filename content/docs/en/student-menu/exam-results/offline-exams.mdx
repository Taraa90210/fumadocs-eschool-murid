The Offline Exam Results feature allows you to view your written/offline exam results that have been graded by teachers. These results include grades per subject, total score, percentage, grade, and pass/fail status.

# Features:

1. **View Your Exam Information**

        At the top of the page, you can see:

        - **Exam Name** - Title of the exam you have taken
        - **Student Name** - Your name

2. **Grade Table Per Subject**

        The table displays detailed grades for each subject tested:

        | Column | Description |
        |--------|-------------|
        | **Sub** | Subject name with type (T) for Theory or (P) for Practical |
        | **Marks** | Score you obtained |
        | **Total** | Maximum total score |
        | **Grade** | Letter grade (A, B, C, D, E) |

        **Example:**
        - Mathematics (T) - 85 / 100 - A
        - Physics (P) - 78 / 100 - B

3. **Total Marks Obtained**

        Card displaying:
        - **Obtained Marks** - Total score you got from all subjects
        - **Total Marks** - Maximum total score from all subjects
        - Format: "Obtained Marks : 425/500"

4. **Percentage and Grade**

        Card displaying:
        - **Grade** - Your overall letter grade (A, B, C, D, E)
        - **Percentage** - Percentage of marks you obtained (example: 85.00%)

5. **Pass/Fail Status**

        Round badge at the bottom displaying:
        - **"Passed"** - With green color if you passed
        - **"Failed"** - With red color if you did not pass
