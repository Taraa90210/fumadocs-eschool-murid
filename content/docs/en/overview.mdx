NoBox.Ai is an artificial intelligence (AI) application designed to improve the quality of marketing and customer service for educational institutions, businesses, and government organizations.  
NoBox helps collect and manage data, broadcast messages, and handle multi-platform chats, powered by an intelligent chatbot that operates 24/7 throughout the year.

## **NoBox.Ai Modules and Advantages**

The modules available in NoBox.Ai include Messages, CRM, Forms, Promotions, Contacts, Account, AI Agents, Subscription, Billing, and Settings.

1. **Messages**  
   A module used to assist customer service teams in handling customer chats and managing messages generated from broadcast campaigns.

2. **CRM**  
   A module used to manage customer interactions, create service- and sales-related information, and improve operational efficiency.

3. **Forms**  
   A module used to input and collect data efficiently.

4. **Promotions**  
   A module designed to run promotions easily and efficiently to targeted audiences, integrated with various channels such as WhatsApp, Telegram, Shopee, and more.

5. **Contacts**  
   A module used to manage promotional target data such as names, phone numbers, email addresses, and other contact details.

6. **Account**  
   A module used to manage account data and NoBox.Ai tools.

7. **AI Agents**  
   A module that enables intelligent automation using AI-powered agents to assist in customer interactions.

8. **Human Agents**  
   A module for managing human customer service agents and their activities.

9. **Subscription**  
   A module for managing subscription plans and service packages.

10. **Billing**  
    A module for managing billing, invoices, and payment history.

11. **Settings**  
    The configuration module of NoBox.Ai, which is divided into several sections:
    - **User Profile**  
      Contains personal information and settings for date format, time format, and number format.
    - **Business Profile**  
      Contains company information, company members, and business working hours settings.
    - **File Manager**
    - **Custom Inputs**
    - **Contact Tools**
