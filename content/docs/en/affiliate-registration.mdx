1. Open the affiliate portal website [click here](https://crm.nobox.ai/affiliate/authentication_affiliate/register)
2. Fill in your personal information
3. Click **\[Register]**
4. Wait for the approval confirmation that will be sent via email
5. Once approved, you can log in using your account
