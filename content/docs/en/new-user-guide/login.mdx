To use the **eSchool Mobile** application, ensure you have an official account registered by the school or an eSchool admin.

### 1. Download the Application

Download the **eSchool Mobile** application via:

* [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=id.ac.eschool.GuruStaff.android\&hl=id) for Android devices
* [Apple App Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=id.ac.eschool.GuruStaff.android\&hl=id) for iOS devices

Search with the keyword: **"eSchool Siswa & Wali"** (eSchool Students & Guardians)

### 2. Login with Official Account

Once the **eSchool Mobile** application is installed, follow these steps to login according to your role:

#### Login as a **Student**

1. Open the **eSchool Mobile** application.
2. Select the **"Login as Student"** option.
3. Enter the login data you received from the school:
   * **School Code**
   * **Registration Number**
   * **Password**
4. After a successful login, you will be directly redirected to the student dashboard page.

#### Login as a **Parent / Guardian**

1. Open the **eSchool Mobile** application.
2. Select the **"Login as Parent"** option.
3. Enter:
   * **Email** (registered with the school)
   * **Password**
4. After logging in, the system will display a list of students connected to your account. Select one to start monitoring their academic activities.

> Login accounts can only be used if they have been **activated by the school admin or eSchool operator**.

***

#### Login Issues?

If you experience problems while logging in, here are the steps you can take:

* [**Forgot Password?**](broken-reference)\
  Use the **"Forgot Password"** feature on the application login page.\
  Follow the reset instructions that will be sent via your registered **email**.
* **Cannot Access Account?**\
  Contact directly:
  * **School Admin** (to check your account status)
  * Or contact the **eSchool Support Team** via the [**Help**](https://www.eschool.ac.id/#contact-us) menu
