### Online Exam Results

The **Online Exam Results** page is designed so that guardians can easily monitor their child's academic performance independently. With informative visuals and a clean layout, guardians can instantly see the scores from each exam the child has taken.

***

#### Information Displayed

* **Subject Name and Exam Title**\
