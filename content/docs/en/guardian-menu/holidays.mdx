**Monitor Child's Holiday Schedule with a Clear and Informative Calendar View**

Through the **Holidays** feature, parents can know the child's holiday schedule directly without needing to ask the school. The system displays holiday information in real-time updated by the school.

**Parent-Friendly Core Features:**

* **Interactive Visual Calendar**\
  Appears with a complete monthly format, accompanied by left and right navigation buttons to switch between months. Dates included as holidays will be given a red visual marker for easy recognition.
* **Holiday Period Information**\
  Below the calendar, detailed holiday periods are available in a complete date range format. For example:\
  `30-07-2025 ~ 07-08-2025`\
  &#xNAN;_Independence Day Preparation Holiday_
* **Clean and Concise Design**\
  The interface is arranged to be easy to read by all parents, including those not accustomed to technology. Each piece of information is displayed with marker icons and different colors for faster recognition.
