#### **Online Exams - Parent Monitoring**

This feature is designed to provide ease for parents in monitoring their child's exam activities online. The exam display is divided into two main categories: **Offline** and **Online**. In this context, users are on the **Online** tab.

**Filters and Navigation**

At the top, several important controls are available to filter and search for exams:

* **Select Subject**: Provides filtering by subject (e.g.: BIG, PKN, BIN).
* **Search Field**: Facilitates searching for exams by name or specific keywords.
* **Status Filter** (second image): Allows users to filter by:
  * **Exam Status**: All, In Progress, Finished, Upcoming.
  * **Participation Status**: All, In Progress, Submitted, Not Taken.

**Online Exam List**

Each exam is displayed in an information card containing:

* **Exam Name**: Such as _Calculus Exam_ or _Odd Semester Physical Fitness Exam_.
* **Subject Category**: Listed at the top of the card (e.g.: MULOK PJOK).
* **Participation Status**: Indicated by badges like **Submitted** (with a green indicator).
* **Exam Date**: Information on when the exam is conducted.
* **Exam Duration**: How long the exam lasts (e.g.: 6 Hours or 2 Hours).

**Feature Objectives**

With this feature, parents can:

* Monitor the child's **online exam history**.
* Know the **participation status** in real-time.
* View the **subject and implementation date** quickly.
* Use filters to focus on exams that are in progress, not taken, or finished.
