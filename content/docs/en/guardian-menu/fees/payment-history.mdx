The **History** page in the **Fees** feature provides a complete recap of all payment transactions made by the user, whether they have been approved or are still pending. Information is presented neatly and is easy to read to help users track the student's education cost contribution history.

#### **Benefits of This Feature**

* Provides transparency and complete documentation of every payment.
* Makes it easier for users (especially parents/guardians) to control school financial status.
* Provides transaction proof that can be accessed anytime if needed for administrative purposes.
