# Overview

### Welcome to eSchool

**Integrated School Management Platform for a Better Future of Education**

***

#### eSchool Overview

**eSchool** is a digital school management platform designed to facilitate the management of learning, academic data, communication, and school activities in one **efficient, structured, and integrated** system.

With a user-friendly interface and complete features, eSchool helps schools and parents build strong collaboration and support modern teaching and learning processes through a mobile application.

***

#### Main Features and Modules

Below are the main features of eSchool divided by user roles:

**Student Portal**

* **Subject Management**\
  Access subject lists, digital learning materials, and file/video downloads.
* **Assignment System**\
  Upload assignments, track status, filter by date/status, and deadline notifications.
* **Attendance & Schedule**\
  Daily attendance, interactive class schedules, and monthly attendance recaps.
* **Exams & Grades**\
  Online exams, grade history, and academic progress reports.
* **Communication**\
  Real-time chat with teachers, school announcements, and push notifications.

**Parent / Guardian Portal**

* **Child Monitoring**\
  Child's academic dashboard, real-time attendance reports, and learning progress.
* **School Payments**\
  View tuition bills, transaction history, proof of payment, and installment systems.
* **Comprehensive Reports**\
  Access grades per subject, performance analysis, and export PDF reports.
* **Communication with School**\
  Direct chat with teachers, academic consultations, and school activity notifications.

**School Features**

* **School Gallery**\
  Documentation of school activities in photo form, with school year filters.
* **Academic Calendar**\
  National holiday schedules, school events, and important activity reminders.
