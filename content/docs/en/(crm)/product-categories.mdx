## **Add Product Category**

To add a new product category, please click **[Products] ➔ [Product Categories] ➔ [Add Product Category]**. The **[Add Product Category]** dialog will appear.

<ImageZoom
  src="/images/id/Tambah%20Kategori.png"
  alt="NoBox Dashboard View"
  width={800}
  height={450}
  className="rounded-lg border shadow-sm mb-4"
/>

Complete the required fields, for example as shown below:

| **Field** | **Description** | **Example** |
| --------- | --------------- | ----------- |
| Name      | Category Name   | Electronics |

After completing the required fields, click **[Save]** to store the newly created product category.

## **Edit and Delete Product Categories**

To edit a product category, first select the category you want to edit. Make the necessary changes and click **[Save]** to save the updated data.

To delete a product category, select the category you want to remove and click **[Delete]**. A confirmation dialog will appear asking whether you want to delete the data. Click **[Yes]** to proceed or **[No]** to cancel.

## **How to Use Product Categories**

1\. Open the **[Products]** menu, then select an existing product or add a new product.

2\. Click the category field, and the list of previously created categories will appear.

3\. Select one of the categories and click **[Save]** to update the product data.
